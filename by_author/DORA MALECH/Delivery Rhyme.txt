



For Alyssa



As anyoneis apt to, you began as someoneelse’s symptom. As inother beginnings: drawn lots, blood,some dancing on the heads of pinsand inside needles’ eyes,cellular revelry,hoppingof microscopicturnstiles. Lucky guest,grist, leaptlong odds to sparkthe tinder in the dark.Then, the subcommittees met:made merry in duplicate, triplicateand so on, much of themselves, dividedand divined and concurred.All sides insides, pre-ambulatoryperambulation meant: sureambit, short orbitin a warm aquarium setto the muffled music of a single sphere.As in other beginnings: parting seas, the future’sviolent egress, screams and sutures,aftermath’s average agonyon umbilical belaybut soon to solo, unfold allthose origami limbs to testthe inevitable debutante bawl.Wrest from the nestand the rest is you, dear:dressed for the bright lightsin bits of my sister.

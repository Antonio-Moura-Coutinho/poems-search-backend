
Dear Aleph,Like Ovid: I’ll have no last words.This is what it means to die among barbarians. Bar bar barwas how the Greeks heard our speech —sheep, beasts — and so we becamebarbarians. We make them revealthe brutes they are, Aleph, by the thingswe make them name. David,they tell me, is the oneone should aspire to, but ever sinceI first heard them say PhilistineI’ve known I am Goliathif I am anything.

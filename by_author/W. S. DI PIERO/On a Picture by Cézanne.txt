
There’s no description in the braided stone,the pear, the stone in the pear, the birchbark,bread hills on the snowfall tablecloth.The dog of work gnaws the day’s short bone,snarls a mountainside into lavender and green.In the mind where objects vanish, almost is all.Element of pitcher, sky, rockface, blank canvasplastic and vast in one off-center patch.To copy what’s invisible, to improvisea soul of things and remake solid lifeinto fresh anxious unlifelike form.

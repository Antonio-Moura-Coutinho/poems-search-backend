
  The truck grinds by and pumps out grit; the road glints and goes still. The barn owl that had not finished herereturns. But withits fill of scavenges, face ruffled in mulch, the vole is lost and safe so the silent specter flits away, its moon face tothe moonand rears unknown against a copse, claws tipped for the strafe and something diestoo soon.

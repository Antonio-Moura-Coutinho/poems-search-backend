
The perfect satisfaction
of wine, cigarettes, the sun
at an afternoon angle
passes through flesh
as if flesh were a sieve
to the direct point
the soul of matter.Things fix timealthough the sun moveslazily, creating an imagethat seems like motivethe wine transmutesand becomes bloodcigarettes dissolveto blue threads and ashbut the sun continuesin constant repetitionof its slow and rather boring dance.
